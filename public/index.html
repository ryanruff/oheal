Oral Health Epidemiology & Analysis Lab at NYU

The Oral Health Epidemiology and Analysis Lab is a research laboratory at the New York University College of Dentistry. Through the lenses of social justice and patient centeredness, OHEAL explores the determinantes of oral health inequity and the testable approahces to mitigate it. The lab comprises a multidisciplinary group of faculty and students across several institutions supporting an innovative methodological approach to dental research, including epidemiologists, clinicians, economists, biostatisticians and health policy/health services researchers.

Oral health and educational performance - A systematic review
Ryan Ruff November 2018

Published article - A systematic review and meta-analysis to provide a summary estimate of the association between oral health and academic performance. Results indicate caries or tooth pain had a negative association with academic achievement and school absenteeism

Economic evaluation of SCPPs
Shulamite Huang August 2018 - Present

This project addresses the need to conduct robust economic evaluations of school-based caries prevention programs (SCPPs) to support decision-making by public health officials, policymakers, and other stakeholders to improve oral health equity.
